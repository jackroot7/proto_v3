How to use bulk upload
1
Download the template
Click "Download CSV Template" above. It includes example rows and all supported columns.
2
Fill in your products
Open in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app. One product per row. Save as .csv or .xlsx.
3
Upload & review
Select your file and upload. New products are created, existing ones (same name) are updated. Errors are shown row by row.
